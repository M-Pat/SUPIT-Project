Algebra University Launches New Data Science Master's Program

December 23, 2020 by Borko

Algebra University College is excited to announce the launch of its new Master's program in Data Science, set to begin in the fall semester of 2024.

This program is designed to equip students with advanced skills in data analysis, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, preparing them for careers in one of the fastest-growing fields. The curriculum, developed in collaboration with industry experts, includes hands-on projects, internships, and opportunities for research.

Interested candidates can attend the upcoming information session on January 15, 2024, to learn more about the program details and admission requirements.

Students Win First Place in International Robotics Competition

December 14, 2020 by Borko

A team of students from Algebra University College has won first place at the prestigious International Robotics Competition held in Berlin.

  • Jan Boran Crncec
  • Domagoj Kuzel
  • Dominik Biskupovic

Competing against universities from around the globe, the team impressed the judges with their innovative design and programming of an autonomous robot capable of complex navigation and task execution. This victory underscores Algebra University's commitment to fostering talent and excellence in STEM fields. The winning team will be sharing their experiences and showcasing their award-winning robot at a special event on campus next week.
